Pancakes

A breakfast classic.

Ingredients

Makes enough for brunch and dinner for one.

  • 225g flour, all-purpose
  • 18g baking powder
  • 6g salt
  • 15g sugar
  • 40g butter
  • 300g milk, I usually use whole
  • 1 large egg

My favorite add-ins and toppings

  • Chocolate chips
  • Strawberries
  • Powdered sugar
  • Peanut butter
  • Cheesecake bites
  • Candy bar pieces
  • M&Ms

Directions

  1. Mix everything together
  2. Add any fillings such as a handful of chocolate chips
  3. Pour pancake sized blobs on pan at medium heat
  4. Flip when bubbles rise to the surface and pop
  5. Wait some more then remove and serve
