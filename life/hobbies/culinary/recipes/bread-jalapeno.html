My Bread Recipe

I learned this recipe from my coworker which I then tweaked to my taste. The recipe isn't very exact but it works pretty well according to my tasting victims volunteers.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 tsp yeast
  • 1.5 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • At least 2 cups flour, probably 3 or 4 but I never measure it

Directions

  1. Mix the yeast, water, and sugar in a bowl
  2. Add the salt and some flour
  3. Gradually add flour and mix until it looks like bread dough
  4. Move it to a floured surface and knead it for a while
  5. Grease a bread pan and place the dough inside
  6. Let it rise in a warm spot for a couple hours until it looks like the iconic bread shape
  7. Preheat an oven to 375° F
  8. Bake for 25 - 45 minutes. Once you smell the bread, check on it. Take it out when golden brown. If it smells burnt, it has been too long.

Variants

  • Jalapeño & Cheese
    • Follow normal directions up to step four
    • Use a rolling pin or your hands to flatten the dough into a rectangle with the same width as the pan and about ½" thick
    • Add your choice of shredded cheese (I recommend cheddar or pepper jack) and some diced jalapeños on it. Pieces of bacon also works well in there.
    • Roll up the dough like a burrito
    • Place it in the greased pan
    • Continue from step six
    • Sprinkle more cheese and jalapeños before baking
  • Cinnamon Swirl
    • Use more sugar during step one, ~¼ cup
    • Follow normal directions up to step four
    • Use a rolling pin or your hands to flatten the dough into a rectangle with the same width as the pan and about 1/2" thick
    • Mix ½ cup sugar and at least 2 tsp of cinnamon in a small bowl
    • Add 3 tbsp melted butter to form a paste
    • Rub the paste on the dough
    • Roll up the dough like a burrito
    • Place it in the greased pan
    • Continue from step six
    • Bake at 350° F
    • Required to serve warm with butter or a little cream cheese frosting
    • To make cinnamon rolls, take the rolled bread, slice it, place the rolls into a pan, let rise then bake
  • Bread Sticks
    • Follow normal directions up to step four
    • Roll the bread flat onto a pan. Can also separate the dough into individual sticks the size of a hot dog.
    • Top with shredded cheese
    • Continue from step six
