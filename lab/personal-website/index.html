Personal Website

My personal and professional website started in my first year of college, February 2018. I began with an online version of my resume that feels like a game. A character of myself strolls through a laboratory, rides a rover, launches a rocket, and lands on Mars. All the while walking by my experience, portfolio, and skills. How many Easter eggs can you find?

Check out the experience

The rest of the website began development in February 2019, and I finished most of it a month later. But like all projects, I am continuously improving it and adding content. I have learned much about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript whilst building it. Furthermore, I have drawn or photographed all artwork, except company logos or explicitly captioned, which has improved my fine art skills.

Thanks Github for hosting it on Github Pages.
